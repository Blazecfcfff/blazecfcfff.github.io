Welcome to the Tropical Paradise Application

Are you passionate about Minecraft and eager to help build an amazing community? This is your chance to join our dedicated staff team!

Before applying, please read the following carefully to ensure you understand what we expect:

  • Commitment: Being a staff member requires time and dedication to keep our server enjoyable and safe.
  • Communication: You must be reachable via Discord and respond in a timely manner.
  • Attitude: Positive attitude and patience are key when dealing with community members.
  • Age: Applications from users under 15 will not be considered..

The application form on the next page will ask you about your experience, your motivation, and how you would contribute to Tropical Paradise. Each required field is marked with an asterisk (*). Optional fields give you a chance to tell us more about yourself.

Once you submit your application, our team will carefully review it and get back to you as soon as possible with further instructions.

Thank you for your interest in joining Tropical Paradise — we’re excited to hear from you!
